## What They Do

Retell AI is a YC W24 voice AI platform that lets businesses deploy AI phone agents for sales, customer support, and appointment scheduling. The platform powers 50M+ real-time AI calls per month for 1,000+ business customers including PWC, Twilio, and healthcare providers. Founded in 2023, they've scaled to $40M+ ARR with a ~25-person team, recently expanding beyond voice into SMS, chat, and email channels.

## Why Photon Fits

Strong platform fit. Retell already delivers multi-channel AI agents across voice, SMS, chat, and email to 1,000+ businesses — but iMessage is completely absent from their channel lineup. Their Jan 2026 press release explicitly positions them as a multi-channel platform ('Deploy Infinite AI Agents Across Voice, Chat, Email and SMS'). Adding iMessage via Photon would fill their most visible US channel gap with zero engineering effort on their side, and every Retell customer would gain iMessage access through a single integration.

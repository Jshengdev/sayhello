## The thesis
**This is not mass-sending slop.** Mass personalization tools blast templated "Hi {firstName}" emails and you
lose your brand to the slop. sayhello is the opposite: it automates the EXPENSIVE, high-value part of
outreach — **crafting a true story about a specific person from public data** — so a human closes with it.
More expensive per lead, yes. That's the point: it's for **agencies and high-touch sellers**, not spray-and-pray.

## Why agencies (the segment)
Agencies and high-touch GTM don't need more volume — they need **a solid scrape that tells a story about
their target and shows exactly how to close**, sales-style. You could hire for X/Y/Z, or you could have a
harness that does the research labor and hands you the angle. The story-driven approach is the only outreach
that survives at the price point where brand matters.

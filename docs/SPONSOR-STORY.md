# SPONSOR STORY — how each sponsor plays its part (for the demo narration + Devpost)

> One line per sponsor: what it does in sayhello + the innovative angle. Point at each as its node lights.

- **Firecrawl — the eyes.** Turns a raw company URL into clean, structured facts. *Innovative:* it's not
  "scrape a page," it's the FIRST evidence layer — every fact it returns becomes a citation the Critic can
  check a claim against. Scraping as grounding, not just data collection.

- **ClickHouse — the truth source.** We query its FREE public datasets (`github_events`, `hackernews`) as
  a live grounding source: star-velocity, release cadence, launch traction. *Innovative:* most teams use
  ClickHouse to STORE their own data; we use its public data as PROOF — a builder-traction signal that
  fires before funding/hiring data shows up. It also archives every generation so the score-climb is queryable.

- **Composio — the reach.** The enrichment action layer: news, SEC filings, finance, firmographics through
  one interface. *Innovative:* a free-first chain (zero-auth COMPOSIO_SEARCH for news/SEC/finance) that only
  escalates to paid lookups once a lead clears a relevance bar. Frugal enrichment.

- **OpenRouter — the two minds.** The drafter writes the story; a DIFFERENT model is the held-out Critic.
  *Innovative:* the agent literally cannot grade its own homework — a different model family judges it, so
  it catches lies the writer is blind to. The whole product hinges on this split.

- **Langfuse — the glass.** Every node is traced; you open the trace of the run you just watched.
  *Innovative:* it's harness observability — you see the agent think, fail, and recover, span by span. The
  honesty of the product is itself made auditable.

- **Thesys C1 / OpenUI — the voice.** The grounded story renders as a generated, adaptive slide UI — per
  lead, no templates. *Innovative:* a data-rich lead becomes a full dashboard, a thin one a lean card; the
  UI shapes itself to each company's story density.

- **Render — the stage.** One Blueprint deploys the whole harness live. *Innovative:* the agent's work
  ships from the same place it runs — the demo URL is the product, public and live.

## The one architecture line for the pitch
"Firecrawl sees it, ClickHouse proves it, Composio reaches for more, two OpenRouter models argue until the
story is true, Langfuse shows the receipts, OpenUI tells it, and Render ships it — a harness that catches
itself lying before you ever hit send."

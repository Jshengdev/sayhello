# THESIS — the underlying value (Johnny's verbatim philosophy, the soul of the Devpost)

## The core insight: a story is denser than a blurb
The information is never the problem; the information is never the solution. **The story is.** A story carries
far more, far more densely, than a giant blurb of data. We don't usually realize that this is exactly what
makes a great GTM person great — it isn't the tools, the automation, or the personalized agents. It's **how
well you understand someone's story, and sell them a pain and a problem out of it.** That's the part that
doesn't scale by spraying. That's the part we built.

## What sayhello actually is
A **GTM-CRM-style scraping layer** that lets an **agent live as a harness on the open web** — it reads a
company and a person from public data, distills their *story* (not a data dump), and hands your sales team the
best possible information to go to market with. A **tag-team duo: you + a co-founder-grade agent.** It doesn't
replace the closer; it arms them with the one thing that makes outreach land — a true understanding of the
other person, grounded so nothing in it is invented.

## Why "sayhello"
Because the whole product is the moment *before* you say hello — knowing their story first. The intro is a
beautiful animation: the word **"say hello"** with the name itself **cycling through all the possible names**
it could be (the many people, the many stories) before settling — then fading into the spiral where one
person's story gets distilled. Say hello → and you already know them.

## The one-line value
**Stories contain more information than blurbs, and sales is won by understanding stories — so we built the
harness that reads a person's true story off the open web and hands it to you to close with.**

## What this unlocks (future of GTM)
GTM stops being volume (spray templated slop, lose your brand) and becomes **signal + story**: every sponsor
in our stack is a piece of the machine that turns public scatter into a grounded human story. The future
isn't more personalized sends — it's deeper *understanding*, verified, at the speed of an agent, with a human
making the call.

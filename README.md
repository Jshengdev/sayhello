# sayhello

A self-improving GTM harness that builds a **grounded** story around a sales lead — and catches the AI
when it invents a fact. Throw an agent at lead research and it hallucinates (a funding round, a customer);
sayhello is the verification harness that refuses to ship a story it can't prove. *Before you say hello, know their story.*

Built for the Harness Engineering Hack (Jun 12 2026). See `CLAUDE.md` → `docs/`.
